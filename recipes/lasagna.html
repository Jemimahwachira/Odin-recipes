LASAGNA RECIPE

INGREDIENTS:

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 400g canned tomato sauce or crushed tomato
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp oregano
  • 250g ricotta or cottage cheese
  • 2 cups shrdded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan (optional)

DIRECTIONS:

  1. Cook the noodles:Boil the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside
  2. Cook the beef:In a pan, saute the onions and garlic. Add ground beef and cook until brown
  3. Add sauce:Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste,salt, and oregano. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Layer the lasagnain a baking dish:
    • spread a bit of meat sauce
    • add noodles
    • Add ricotta/cottage cheese
    • Add mozzarella
    • Repeat layers
  5. Top with remaining cheese
  6. Bakeat 180 degrees celcius for 30-40 mins until bubbly and golden
  7. Let rest for 10 mins before serving

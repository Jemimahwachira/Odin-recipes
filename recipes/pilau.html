PILAU RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups rice (washed)
  • 400g beef (cubed)
  • 1 large onion (sliced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1tsp ginger (grated)
  • 2 tsp pilau masala
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • Salt to taste
  • 31/2 cups water
  • Oil for cooking

DIRECTIONS:

  1. In a pot, fry the onions in oil until dark brown (not burnt)
  2. Add garlic, ginger, and pilau masala. Stir for 30 seconds.
  3. Add the beef and cook until browned
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft
  5. Add rice and mix well
  6. Add water and salt.Bring to a boil
  7. Cover and simmer on low heat until rice is fully cooked (about 20-25 mins)
  8. Fluff with fork and serve hot

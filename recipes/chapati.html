Chapati recipe

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water (adjust as needed)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp oil or melted butter (for the dough)
  • extra oil for cooking

DIRECTIONS

  1. Mix flour, salt, and sugar in a bowl
  2. Add oil and warm water gradually while kneading into a soft douh
  3. Knead for about 10 minutes. Cover and let rest for 30 minutes
  4. Divide dough into equal balls (about 8)
  5. Roll out each ball into circle
  6. Heat pan and cook each chapati with a little oil until golden on both sides
  7. Stack them in a clean cloth to keep soft
